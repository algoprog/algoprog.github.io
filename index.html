Biography

Hello, I’m a research assistant at National University of Singapore doing research in natural language processing, with applications in the detection of misinformation. I’m also a Machine Learning Consultant at Ro5, working on the automated construction of a biomedical knowledge graph that will assist in drug discovery.

Previously, I was at Plural AI, where I worked on entity extraction from webpages and semantic search, and at Donaco, where I worked on contextual recommendation of charity campaigns.

As a researcher, my goal is to help overcome the limitations of current machine learning models (efficiency, robustness, limited training resources), and improve the ways knowledge is a) discovered by people, and b) understood and exploited by ML models.
